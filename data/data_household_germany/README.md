## Overview

This dataset comes from the paper "Dataset on electrical single-family house and heat pump load profiles in Germany" (https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-022-01156-1)

Given variables:
Column header | Unit | Description
--- | --- | ---
S | VA | Apparent power
P | W | Active power, the relevant metric indicating the power generated by the solar panel
Q | VAR | Reactive power

**Chosen PV system for the analysis: South.**
It consists of 58 modules, each with a nominal power of 250 W, resulting in a total nominal power of 14.5 kW. The system is located near (in the same district as) the single family households whose load profiles are provided in the dataset.

**Datetime**
The 2019_data_15min.hdf5 file contains a datetime index with a 15-min in the format: `Seconds since 01.01.1970
00:00:00 UTC`.
